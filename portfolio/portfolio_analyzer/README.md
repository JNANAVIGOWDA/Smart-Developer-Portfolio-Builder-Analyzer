# 🚀 DevAnalyzer: Smart Developer Portfolio Builder

Welcome to your professional portfolio project! This guide will help you run and manage the project without any help.

---

## 🛠️ Step 1: Start the Servers (XAMPP)
Before opening the project, you must start your local servers:
1. Open the **XAMPP Control Panel**.
2. Click **Start** next to **Apache**.
3. Click **Start** next to **MySQL**.
4. Ensure both are highlighted in **green**.

---

## 🌐 Step 2: Accessing the Project
Open your web browser and use the following links:

*   **Login Page**: [http://localhost:8080/login.html](http://localhost:8080/login.html)
*   **Create/Edit Portfolio**: [http://localhost:8080/form.html](http://localhost:8080/form.html)
*   **Your Live Portfolio**: [http://localhost:8080/portfolio.php?user_id=3](http://localhost:8080/portfolio.php?user_id=3)

---

## 📱 Step 3: Sharing with Others
### **On your home/office Wi-Fi:**
To show your portfolio to someone on the same Wi-Fi (like on your phone), use this link:
`http://192.168.29.204:8080/portfolio.php?user_id=3`

### **To the whole world:**
To share with everyone on the internet, you need to upload these files to a "Web Host" like **000webhost** or **Hostinger**.

---

## 📁 Project Structure
*   `portfolio.php`: The main page your visitors see.
*   `form.html`: The page where you fill in your details and projects.
*   `dashboard.php`: Your private area to see your analysis scores.
*   `css/style.css`: Controls the "Premium" look and theme.
*   `uploads/`: This is where your profile photos and resumes are stored.

---

## 🆘 Troubleshooting
*   **Blank Page?**: Make sure Apache is running in XAMPP.
*   **Database Error?**: Make sure MySQL is running in XAMPP.
*   **Link not working?**: Check if your IP address has changed by running `ipconfig` in your terminal.
